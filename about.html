About the Health Test

Methodology & COVID-19

This website intelligently takes in data about your symptoms and cross-references it with information in our medical database to suggest a diagnosis based on your symptoms. In light of the global COVID-19 pandemic, the app is geared towards identifying COVID-19 symptoms first, and ensuring that those with these symptoms are informed of them as soon as possible, to prevent further infection.

Referral

Upon determining what the potential diagnosis may be, and based on the severity of the outcome, the website decides between suggesting home remedies/treatments or referring you to nearby medical facilities. This provides clarity to users on whether their symptoms are manageable from home or whether they should seek further advice.

N.B: Information obtained from this website is not a substitute for a medical diagnosis from a certified medical professional. Please ensure you gain professional medical advice if your symptoms persist or worsen. Health-Test claims no responsibility for information obtained through this service.

Usability

in order to best mimic the question-answer format that a doctor's appointment usually takes, Health-Test uses a series of simple yes-no questions prompted to the user to ask them about the conditions they have been facing, before applying their answers to conclude: Whether the condition is present and, if so, the severity of the condition.

It then uses this information to make a recommendation to the user.
